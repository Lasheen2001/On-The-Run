Frequenly Asked Questions



How do I open an account?
To open an account, you can visit any of our branches with your valid ID and proof of address or by logging into your account and selecting add Bank Account.
What is the minimum balance for an account?
The minimum balance for a savings account is $100, and for a checking account is $500.
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions.
How do I transfer funds between accounts?
You can transfer funds between your accounts by logging into your account and selecting the "Transfer" option from the menu.
What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
If your card is lost or stolen, please contact our customer service immediately to report the issue and block your card.
What are the fees for using an ATM?
We do not charge any fees for using our ATMs. However, if you use an ATM outside of our network, there may be additional fees charged by the ATM owner.
How do I enroll in online banking?
To enroll in online banking, you can typically visit the bank's website and look for a "Sign Up.
What is the daily withdrawal limit for my account?
The daily withdrawal limit for your account may vary depending on the type of account you have and the bank's policies. You can usually find information about your withdrawal limit by checking your account agreement or contacting your bank's customer service.
How do I apply for a loan?
To apply for a loan, you can typically visit your bank's website, visit a branch in person, or call the bank's customer service. You'll need to provide some personal and financial information, such as your income, employment history, and credit score, and specify the type of loan you're interested in.
What is the interest rate on my savings account?
The interest rate on your savings account may vary depending on the bank's policies and current market conditions. You can usually find information about your account's interest rate by checking your account agreement or contactingyour bank's customer service.
How can I view my account balance and transaction history?
You can view your account balance and transaction history by logging into your online banking account or by visiting a bank branch and speaking with a teller.
How do I set up automatic bill payments?
To set up automatic bill payments, you can usually log into your online banking account and look for a "Bill Pay" or "Payments" section. From there, you can enter the payment information for the bills you want to pay automatically
